Activities and Things to Do In Pembrokeshire

Below is a small example of the many varied activities and things to do in Pembrokeshire.

Pembroke Activity Centre – 01646 622013
Offering an unrivaled choice of land-based and on-the-water activities, the Pembrokeshire Activity Centre is the place to get active with such a wide range of activities.

Outer Reef Surf School – 01646 680070
A local surf school who offer lessons on Freshwater West.

Oakwood Theme Park – 01834 891376
One of Wales’s best theme parks a great day out for the entire family

Folly Farm – 01834 812731
‘Best Family Day Out in Wales’ award winners at the National Tourism Awards for Wales 2010-2012

Manor House Wildlife Park – 01646 651201
Experience close-up meetings with endangered animals from all over the world.

Ocean Commotion Play Center – 01834 845526
Ocean Commotion is a family run indoor children’s playcentre located in the heart of the walled town of Tenby.

Heatherton Leisure Park – 01646 652000
A great family leisure park which has activities like paintball, karting, golf, archery and horse riding.

Carew Karting – 07974 540689
Great fun for all the family 15 minutes practice sessions for races over one and a half hours. Races are electronically lap scored.

Pemberton Chocolate Factory – 01994 448800
There’s 3000 years of history in chocolate and a tour of the factory is available. You may even get some free samples.

Caldey Island – 01834 844453
It is one of Britain’s holy islands. The Cistercian monks of Caldey continue a tradition which began there in Celtic times.

Pembrokeshire Riding Center – 01646 682513
A great local riding school, a very short drive away and they offer horse riding lessons from beginners upwards.

Coastal Walks In Pembrokeshire – 01437 776313
A useful resource listing many websites which detail the various walks and greenways throughout Pembrokeshire.

Coastal Bus Service – 01437 764551
The are many bus services which run along the coast, the coastal cruiser number 387 stops at the end of the village.

Sea Fishing From Tenby – 07968 480006
The Mackerel fishing trips are enjoyed by both adults and children alike and is a great day out.

Whale and Dolphin Watching – 01437 721721
Boat trips to Ramsey Island RSBP nature reserve for whale and dolphin watching as well as exciting jet boat trips.

South Pembrokeshire Golf Course – 01646 621453
A great local 18 hole golf course described as “a fair and challenging course” for golfers.

Pembroke Castle – 01646 684585
Pembroke Castle offers a fantastic day out in South Wales for the old and young alike.

Skomer Island – 01656 724100
Skomer Island is an absolutely stunning National Nature Reserve off the Pembrokeshire coast.

Beaches

Pembrokeshire county has the largest range of Blue Flag Beaches or Seaside Award beaches than any other county in the whole of Britain. There are over 50 beaches to chose from many of which are award winning.
