The Granary

The Granary has pretty bedrooms with exposed A-Frames which complement the wooden floors and whitewashed walls. There are distant sea views over farm land from the main bedroom which has a king size bed.

There is a large family bathroom with bath and separate large shower compartment. The twin bedroom is very spacious with a smart television (with Netflix). There are also sea views from this room. There is ample storage with fitted wardrobes and chest of drawers in both bedrooms.

In the large open plan living area there are feature arched windows with exposed brick surround. The floor has been laid with reclaimed Welsh slate tiles with under floor heating to complement the wood burning stove. The Granary is equipped to a high standard. There is an electric range cooker, large fridge freezer, microwave, dishwasher, washing machine and tumble dryer. There is a walk in larder and an ample range of cooking utensils. A high chair is available. There is a smart television (with Netflix). The Granary is more like a second home than a holiday let. There is a fantastic range of activities, stunning beaches and fabulous places to eat to complement your stay at The Granary.

The pretty landscaped private patio for the exclusive use of The Granary has a built in barbeque and seating area which has outside lighting. There is a large lawn of 3/4 of an acre which is shared with The Byre.
