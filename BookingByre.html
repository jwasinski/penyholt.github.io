Byre Pricing

Below is a pricing table for The Byre. The rates for the colours are as follows:
Yellow: £440, Orange £500, Green £540, Blue £700, Purple £800, Red £950.

The change over date is Friday, short breaks available by request. 
Off street parking: 1 car.

Facilities: Electric ceramic hob and oven, microwave, large fridge/freezer, slimline dishwasher, washing machine, tumble dryer, 2 TVs, DVD player, PS2, radio, CD player, iPod Dock, bed linen, towels, barbecue, garden furniture.

Dogs are welcome at £10 per week. Wireless Internet is available for free.

Please note that this property is set in a unique location several miles from Castlemartin military range. The range covers an area of 2400 hectares on the South Pembrokeshire Coast within the Pembrokeshire Coast National Park.
