Pubs and Restaurants In Pembrokeshire

There are many restaurants and great family pubs scattered throughout Pembrokeshire. Below is just a small sample of them.

Stackpole Inn, Stackpole
An award winning restaurant voted “Best food experience in Pembrokeshire 2011″.

The Old Point House, Angle
The Old Point House an historic, beautiful, and charming coastal pub set on the shores of tranquil East Angle Bay.

The Brewery Inn, Cosheton
The Brewery Inn has great food especially it's Sunday lunch and has a weekly pub quiz.

Ultracomida, Narberth
A very good cheese shop and deli with a tapas restaurant at the back of the shop.
